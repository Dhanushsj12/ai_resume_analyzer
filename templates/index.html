Questions

How does the analysis work?

Gemini AI reads your resume, checks ATS compatibility, compares it against the job description you provide, and returns concrete suggestions — not a vague score with no explanation.

What file format is supported?

PDF only, for now. Export your resume as a PDF before uploading.

Is my resume stored?

No. Your file is processed in memory for the analysis and is not kept afterward.

Do I need an account?

No sign-up. Upload, paste the job description, and get your results in the same session.
